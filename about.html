Our Story

Learn about our mission to transform the dining experience and create meaningful connections through food.

Our Mission

Hopps was created to promote the joy of dining out, café hopping, and socializing in vibrant settings. It is not just another discovery or booking app—it's a dynamic social experience, combining elements of convenience, community, and personalization.

Hopps is the ultimate destination for those who love exploring new places and making decisions easy in groups. By blending discovery, booking, and social interaction, Hopps takes your dining experience to the next level.

The Challenge

Whether dining solo or planning a group outing, deciding where to go can be a frustrating experience.

For individuals, discovering the perfect place to match your mood or taste often requires scrolling through multiple apps, sifting through irrelevant information.

When in groups, it's even harder—coordinating everyone's preferences feels impossible, and agreeing on a venue can turn into a tedious task.

The Solution

Hopps is designed to make going out as seamless as possible, whether you're dining alone or with friends. It combines advanced discovery tools with an intuitive, social decision-making experience.

Personalized Discoveries

Get real-time recommendations for nearby restaurants, cafes, pubs, and bars based on your preferences. Whether you're craving a particular dish or looking for a trendy café to work from, Hopps has you covered.

Stay Updated

Follow your favorite venues to stay in the loop about upcoming events, shows, and special promotions. Receive updates directly from business owners so you never miss out on live performances, exclusive offers, or themed nights.

Plan a Hopp

Planning a group outing? Use the "Plan a hopp" feature under the "Plan" tab. Each group member can suggest venues using a fun, interactive swiping mechanism, making it easier to reach a consensus.

AI-Driven Suggestions

Not sure where to go or what you're in the mood for? Hopps' AI chatbot can assist. Simply type in your requirements and get an instant, curated list of options to match your mood.

Hassle-Free Bookings

Once you've made a decision, you can seamlessly book your table directly through the app, ensuring that you always have a spot at your favorite venues.

Discover New Recipes

Explore delicious recipes, including exciting cocktail and mocktail creations. Learn how to recreate your favorite drinks at home or get inspired for your next gathering.
