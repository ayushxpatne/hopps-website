Privacy Policy

Your trust matters to us. Learn how we protect your personal information and respect your privacy.

Effective Date: March 15, 2025

At Hopps, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the information you provide when using the Hopps app and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

a. Personal Information

When you sign up or use our services, we may collect personal information such as:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Profile Picture (if uploaded)

b. Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the app, including:

  • App Usage Data (e.g., pages viewed, features used)
  • Location Data (if you grant permission)

c. Content and Communications

  • Messages and feedback you share within the app
  • Images and files you upload to the platform

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve Hopps' features and services
  • To personalize content and recommendations
  • To enable location-based recommendations and services
  • To respond to user inquiries and provide customer support
  • To monitor and enhance the app's performance and security
  • For marketing and promotional purposes (with your consent)

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

a. Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party providers who help us operate the app (e.g., hosting, analytics, customer support).

b. Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

c. Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

4. Your Choices and Control

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your profile information within the app
  • Delete Account: You can request to delete your account and data through the app settings
  • Notifications: You can opt in or out of push notifications at any time
  • Location Permissions: You can enable or disable location tracking through your device settings

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. When data is no longer needed, we securely delete it.

6. Security Measures

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission
  • Secure storage of data with access controls
  • Regular security audits and monitoring

7. Third-Party Links and Services

Hopps may contain links to third-party websites and services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties.

8. Children's Privacy

Hopps is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:

support@hopps.in

Have Questions About Our Policy?

Our support team is ready to assist you with any privacy-related concerns you might have.
